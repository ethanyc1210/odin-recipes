Air Fryer Chicken

Description

This dish is extremely easy to make. It is delicious and diet friendly. Within only 16 minutes, you'll have your delicious chicken ready for anyone!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sauce
  • Your favorite spices

Steps

  1. Prepare and marinate the chicken as you like.
  2. Set air fryer to 375 degree Fahrenheit.
  3. Start frying for 10 minutes on one side.
  4. Pause the air fryer, fry the other side for additional 6 minutes.
  5. When time is up, let the chicken sit for 2 more minutes.
  6. Chop it whichever way you like and enjoy the delicious dish!
